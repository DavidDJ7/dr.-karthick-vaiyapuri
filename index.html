Meet Dr. Karthick Vaiyapuri

Arthroscopy and Joint Replacement Surgeon

Dr. Karthick Vaiyapuri is one of the leading Arthroscopy and Joint Replacement Surgeons practicing in Trichy. Dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge, he has garnered experience through several live and cadaveric workshops.

Following up on the interest he developed during his postgraduate studies, he did his Fellowship in Arthroscopy and Arthroplasty from Amrita Institute of Medical Sciences, Kochi.

He has also completed a fellowship specialising in Shoulder Arthroscopy and Sports Medicine under Professor Dr. Jaechul Yoo, Samsung Medical Centre, South Korea.
